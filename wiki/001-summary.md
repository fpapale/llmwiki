### 4. Emerging Themes  

- **Artificial perception** – Rosenblatt’s 1958 perceptron foreshadowed modern neural networks capable of “perceiving, recognizing and identifying” without explicit human training.  
- **Future mobile data** – Exponential growth continues; forecasts (Statista, 2021) show massive increases in daily data traffic per user.  
- **Societal impact** – Speculative scenarios (e.g., “What if the Titanic sank today?”) illustrate how ICT reshapes communication, emergency response, and even events like papal elections.  

---  

**Take‑away:**  
The ICT landscape has progressed from simple wired telegraphy to a hyper‑connected world where data, computation, and communication converge in real time. Understanding this evolution is essential for modern signal‑processing, machine‑learning, and dynamic‑spectrum‑access research.
