### Milestones in ICT History

- **1837:** Invention of the telegraph by Samuel F. B. Morse.
- **1865:** Electromagnetic field theory by James Clerk Maxwell.
- **1871-1876:** Telephone patents by Alexander Graham Bell and Antonio Meucci.
- **1894:** First radio experiments by Guglielmo Marconi.
- **1928:** Fully electronic television by Philo Taylor Farnsworth and Vladimir Kosma Zworykin.
- **1948:** Birth of Information Theory by Claude Shannon.
- **1973:** Introduction of the cellular phone by Martin Cooper.
- **1990:** Emergence of Wireless Internet.
